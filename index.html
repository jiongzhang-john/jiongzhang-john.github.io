I am currently an associate professor of the Group of Intelligent Medical Imaging (iMED) at Institute of Biomedical Engineering, Ningbo Institute of Materials Technology and Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I have obtained my PhD degree from the IMAG/e group at Eindoven University of Technology (TU/e), Eindhoven, the Netherlands in 2017. Afterwards, I worked as a postdoctoral research associate at TU/e. From 2018 I moved to the US and continued my postdoctoral research at Laboratory of NeuroImaging (LONI) at Keck School of Medicine, University of Southern California (USC), Los Angeles, USA. My research interests include AI-based ophthalmic image processing, ophthalmic microvascular and cerebrovascular analysis, shape analysis, and neuroimage analysis. In the last few years, I have published over 70 papers in leading medical imaging journals and conferences, including IEEE TMI, IEEE TIP, MedIA, IEEE TBME, PR, MICCAI and ISBI. I have been actively involved in many academic services including the Publicity Chair of ICCCV (2022), Program Committee of AAAI (2022), CVPR workshop CVPM (2019-2023), ICCV workshop CVPM (2019) , MICCAI workshop OMIA (2017-current), VSIP (2021) and etc.
My research focuses on:
  • Ophthalmic Image Processing: image enhancement, pathology segmentation/detection, image registration.
  • Vascular Analysis: retinal microvascular segmentation and analysis (2D/3D), cerebrovascular segmentation analysis.
  • Shape Analysis: vascular/cortical surface reconstruction and analysis.
  • ⚡Related Diseases: diabetic retinopathy/neuropathy (DR/DN), age-related macular degeneration (AMD), glaucoma, alzheimer's disease (AD), ischemic stroke, and moyamoya disease.
  • ⚡Image Modalities: [Eye] OCT, AS-OCT, OCTA, FA, Fundus, UWF, Confocal Microscopy (CM). [Brain] MRI, MRA, CTA, DSA.
